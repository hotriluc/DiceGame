?

- The game has 2 players, playing in rounds

- In each turn, a player rolls dices. Each result is added to player ROUND score

- BUT, if the player rolls a 1 (one/both dices with 1), player's ROUND score will be set to 0. After that, it's the next player's turn

- IF the player rolls 6 in the current and the next round (double six ALLOWED in the current round), player's ROUND score will be set to 0. After that, it's the next player's turn

- The player can choose to 'Hold', which means that his ROUND score gets added to his GLOBAL score. After that, it's the next player's turn

- The first player to reach 100 points (by default, can be changed) on GLOBAL score wins the game

- You are able to set N points to WIN into the input field, after entering the value press NEW Game
